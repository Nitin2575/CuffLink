# Design Direction

CuffLink should feel clean, premium, minimal, and social.

Design principles:

- Fast to use
- Easy to remember
- Low friction
- Human, not corporate
- Built for real-world interaction
- More elegant than a business card
- More contextual than a LinkedIn connection

Possible visual language:

- Black, white, silver, deep blue
- Minimal profile cards
- Apple Wallet-inspired passes
- Event-style identity badges
- Map pins and connection clusters
- Watch and iPhone interaction visuals
