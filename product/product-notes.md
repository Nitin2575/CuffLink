# Product Notes

CuffLink is an iPhone-first contextual networking app.

The core product insight is that people do not just need a better way to exchange contact information. They need a better way to remember the context of how and why they met someone.

The first version should stay simple:

- Digital profile
- QR code
- Wallet pass
- Context stamp
- Follow-up reminder
- Basic tags

Long-term, CuffLink can explore interactive connection maps, event-based networking, personal CRM features, and watchOS/wearable add-ons.
