# AI Ideas

Possible AI features:

- Auto-generated follow-up suggestions
- Contact memory summaries
- Event recap notes
- Suggested tags
- Relationship reminders
- Conversation note cleanup
- Personal CRM insights
- Map-based connection summaries
- Community interaction insights

AI should not be the core product at first.

The core product is contextual connection capture.

AI can later help users remember, organize, and follow up on the people they meet.
